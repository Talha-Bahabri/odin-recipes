Lasagna

Rich and delicous ingredients stacked upon each other

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Meat
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. mince the meat and cook it with the tomato sauce
  2. cook the pasta a little bit
  3. now divide your pasta,sauce and cheese to 3 parts
  4. layer tomato sauce first follwer by the pasta , finally the cheese
  5. repeat till finished
