Kabsa

Simple and dilicous cooked rice with chicken

Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • onion
  • tomato
  • potato

Steps

  1. Cook the onion then add the tomato
  2. add the potato and cook it a little bit
  3. add the chicken and give it a good sear
  4. add the rice (1 cup rice for 2 cups water) to the pot
  5. cook it on low heat till the rice is tender
  6. let it rest for 15 min and enjoy
